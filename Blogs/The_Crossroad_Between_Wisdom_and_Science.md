## Rooted in Ancient Knowledge, Refined by Modern Science

Traditional fermentation methods have been practiced for millennia, originally developed by Buddhist monks as a way to preserve food while enhancing its medicinal properties. In many cultures, including within TCM, fermentation was seen not just as a preservation method but as a way to cultivate vital energy in foods, enriching them with enzymes, high bioavailable peptides and probiotics that support the body’s natural balance.

Through fermentation, raw ingredients undergo a transformation, wherein beneficial microorganisms break down nutrients and convert them into forms that are easier for the body to absorb. This process also generates enzymes that aid digestion, reduce inflammation, boost immunity, and support detoxification, making it a powerful tool in promoting health. However, the potential of fermentation has been largely untapped by modern society until recent advancements in biotechnology enabled us to study and optimize these ancient practices.

At ZenCleanz, we have taken this ancient practice and paired it with the latest biotechnological advancements. By doing so, we are able to create enzyme therapies that not only honor traditional methods but also benefit from the precision and safety afforded by modern research and cutting-edge facilities meeting the ISO22000, HACCP AND GMP standards. Our approach is guided by the ever-growing demand for natural self-care, as well as the burgeoning interest in self-regeneration through whole-body detoxification and nourishment.

While this theory has roots in ancient health practices, it has also found a resurgence in the field of modern medicine. By focusing on optimizing the body's internal environment rather than just targeting external pathogens, we are able to address the root causes of many modern health issues.

This philosophy underpins the development of our “5 ELEMENT DETOX & REJUVENATION SYSTEM,” a holistic, plant-based ultra fermented enzymes approach to self-regeneration. Designed for individuals who are health-conscious or who fall into the category of “sub-healthy” populations, those in prey of chronic health issues and also those who may not suffer from serious illness but feel below their optimal health, this system offers a pragmatic solution for rejuvenation. Our products, based on naturally fermented plant-based ingredients, offer the nutrients and enzymes necessary to cleanse the body, restore balance, and rejuvenate vitality.

## Traditional Fermentation: A Time-Honored, Chemical-Free Process

When we talk about traditional fermentation, we refer to a process that has been passed down through generations, a practice that has stood the test of time for its ability to enhance the nutritional and therapeutic value of food. Unlike modern commercial fermentation techniques, which often use artificial accelerants, our fermentation process is slow, organic, and chemical-free. This ensures that the enzymes we produce are as concentrated, potent, and effective as possible.

By utilizing organic, seasonal ingredients and extending the fermentation period over months or even years, we are able to produce enzymes with optimal bioavailability, the measure of how effectively a substance can be absorbed and utilized by the body. The result is a product that is not only rich in nutrients and healing compounds but also vibrationally active, helping to rejuvenate the body at the cellular level.

Our products are thick, flavorful, and packed with beneficial enzymes that promote healthy digestion, reduce inflammation, boost immunity, and support detoxification. Integrating these products into your daily routine can help restore balance, increase energy, and contribute to overall wellness.

### ZenCleanz’s Three Core Values

At ZenCleanz, we are driven by three core values that guide every aspect of our business:

**Nutrient-Rich Ingredients**: All of our raw materials are grown organically and allowed to ripen naturally. More than 150 types of seasonal vegetables, fruits, herbs, mushrooms, and seaweeds are used in our formulas.

**Patented Starter Bacteria**: We use a patented starter bacteria that is changed every five generations, ensuring that our enzymes remain highly active and effective. This is a practice that sets us apart from industry standards, where bacteria are often used for up to 14 generations.

**Traditional Fermentation Method**: Each ingredient is fermented separately through a three-stage process before being blended and fermented again according to specific formulas. This process can extend over three years, ensuring the highest level of enzymatic activity and potency.

These core values reflect our commitment to excellence, purity, and the long-term health of our customers.
