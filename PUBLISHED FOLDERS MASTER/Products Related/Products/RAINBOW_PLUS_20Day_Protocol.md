## Frequently Asked Questions (20-Day Rainbow Plus Protocol)

### What products are included in the 20-Day Rainbow Plus Protocol?

The Rainbow Plus Protocol includes **2 ONE kits**, **3 FORGIVE kits**, 1 Gut Essentials Kit (30 pack), 1 HYGIEIA (750ml), 1 AMBROSIA (750ml), and 1 LIGHTSEED (750ml).

### How long does the 20-Day Rainbow Plus Protocol take?

The Rainbow Plus Protocol is a **20-day** transformational enzymatic journey with multiple intestinal cleanses and three liver flushes for deeper detoxification.

### What is the difference between Rainbow Plus (20-Day) and Rainbow (7-Day)?

Rainbow is a **7-day** protocol with **one** intestinal cleanse and **one** liver flush. Rainbow Plus is a **20-day** intensive with **two** intestinal cleanses, **three** liver flushes, and additional liquid enzymes (LIGHTSEED and full-size AMBROSIA) for deeper transformation.

### Why choose the 20-Day Rainbow Plus over the 7-Day Rainbow?

The Rainbow Plus Protocol is designed for those ready to go deeper with higher enzyme dosages, multiple flushes, and sustained therapeutic nourishment. It provides more thorough cleansing of both the digestive tract and biliary system.

### What should I avoid during the 20-Day Rainbow Plus Protocol?

During the detox, avoid smoking, alcohol, caffeine, fiber, fat, and sugars including natural sugars like carrots, beets, and honey. Stick to a liquid diet between cleanse days.

### Can I do the 20-Day Rainbow Plus Protocol if I'm pregnant or nursing?

**Pregnant:** The ONE and FORGIVE detoxes are not advisable for pregnant women, though other liquid enzymes and sachets are safe.

**Nursing:** The protocol can be used with discretion, or milk can be pumped beforehand and stored while pumping and dumping during the detox.

### What should I do if I experience detox symptoms?

Detox headaches, heartburn, and nausea are common symptoms of toxins moving through the body and will pass. Helpful tools include castor oil packs (NOT on flush days), heating pads, abdominal massage, more warm water, and light movement like walking.
