## Frequently Asked Questions (7-Day Rainbow Protocol)

### What products are included in the 7-Day Rainbow Protocol?

The 7-day Rainbow Protocol combines the deep intestinal sweep of ONE, liver and bile flow restoration of FORGIVE, microbiome rebalance of the Gut Essentials, and the liquid enzymatic life-force of HYGIEIA.

### How long does the 7-Day Rainbow Protocol take?

The Rainbow Protocol is a **7-day** synergistic journey that activates every organ of digestion and detox through a structured daily schedule.

### What is the difference between Rainbow (7-Day) and Rainbow Plus (20-Day)?

Rainbow is a **7-day** protocol with **one** intestinal cleanse and **one** liver flush. Rainbow Plus is a **20-day** intensive with **two** intestinal cleanses, **three** liver flushes, and additional liquid enzymes (LIGHTSEED and AMBROSIA) for deeper transformation.

### What should I avoid during the 7-Day Rainbow Protocol?

During the detox, avoid smoking, alcohol, caffeine, fiber, fat, and sugars including natural sugars like carrots, beets, and honey. Stick to a liquid diet after the ONE intestinal cleanse.

### Can I do the 7-Day Rainbow Protocol if I'm pregnant or nursing?

**Pregnant:** The ONE and FORGIVE detoxes are not advisable for pregnant women, though other liquid enzymes and sachets are safe.

**Nursing:** The protocol can be used with discretion, or milk can be pumped beforehand and stored while pumping and dumping during the detox.

### What should I do if I experience detox symptoms like headaches or nausea?

Detox headaches, heartburn, and nausea are common symptoms of toxins moving through the body and will pass. Helpful tools include castor oil packs (NOT on Day 6), heating pads, abdominal massage, more warm water, and light movement like walking.

## Preparation Tips for 7-Day Rainbow Protocol

### Before Starting:

- Stop smoking and do not drink alcohol or caffeine for the duration of the detox
- Eating lightly for a day or two beforehand can be beneficial‚Äîchoose easily digestible foods such as soups, fruits, and steamed vegetables
- Meat tends to take longer to pass through the digestive system
- Read through the entire checklist and FAQs before attempting the protocol
- Make sure all contents are in the kit
- Lay out all packets and liquid enzymes in order of the schedule. **Take them in order.**
- Keep a notebook journal dedicated to detoxing

### During and After:

- We **strongly recommend** keeping to a liquid diet for the days after the ONE intestinal cleanse so your body may continue to release plaque
- If you begin eating, the body goes into "digestion mode." Only in a fasted state will your body easily release plaque
- If you feel hungry, we suggest more ZenCleanz liquid enzymes, thin broths, miso, raw fresh green juices (no fruit or fiber), and weak herbal tea
- Avoid fiber, fat, and sugars including natural sugar ingredients such as carrots, beets, honey, etc.
- You may drink more warm water throughout the day
- On **Day 6**, we recommend only sips within 2 hours of the flush drink
- Cool or cold water can shock the body; warm water helps disperse the enzymes
- Take any necessary medication at least 1 hour away from the enzymes
