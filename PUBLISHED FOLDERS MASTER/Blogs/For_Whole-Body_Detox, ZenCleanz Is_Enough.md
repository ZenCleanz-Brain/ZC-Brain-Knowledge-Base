### Detox Is Not One Mechanism. It Is a System.

The human body does not detox through a single organ or a single ingredient. It detoxes through a coordinated system of drainage pathways, all working together. The digestive tract processes and eliminates incoming load. The liver and bile transform and package waste. The blood and lymph transport metabolic byproducts. The lungs and skin release volatile wastes. The kidneys filter and excrete what no longer belongs.

And beyond the physical body, there is more. Emotional tension alters circulation and organ tone. Chronic stress disrupts digestion, elimination, and repair. Mental overload drains vitality and slows recovery.

Detox, in reality, is whole-body, whole-being physiology.

ZenCleanz was built to support all of it at once, rather than asking the body to handle detox in fragments.
