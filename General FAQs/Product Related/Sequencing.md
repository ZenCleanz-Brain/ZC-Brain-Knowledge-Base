### How the Time Actually Adds Up

## Foundational sequencing (must be respected in order)

---

## These phases are mostly sequential and set the base.

**Phase 1 (enzymatic entry)**: ~0.5–1 month

**Phase 2 (digestive drainage)**: ~0.75–1.5 months

**Phase 3 (liver & bile)**: ~1–2 months

---

### ➡️ Subtotal: ~ 3–5 months

At this point, the body is no longer congested and can safely multitask.

### Mid-level detox (begins to overlap)

## These phases often overlap with each other once drainage is restored.

**Phase 4 (lungs & skin)**: ~0.75–1.5 months

**Phase 5 (blood & lymph)**: ~1–2 months

**Phase 6 (kidneys & reserves)**: ~2–4 months

---

### ➡️ These do not add fully on top of each other.

### They typically unfold over ~4–6 months total.

### Terrain restoration (long arc, background phase)

### Phase 7 (terrain restoration): 6–18 months

**This phase**: 

### Starts before the others are “finished”

Continues while living normal life

Is not felt as “detox” anymore

Is where stability, resilience, and sovereignty are built

### If done intelligently and gently:

### Active detox journey: ~ 7–10 months

### Full biological consolidation: ~ 12–24 months
